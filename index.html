What News Patrol do?

With the advent of technology, information is free for everyone. This is an advancement in human history, but at the same time it blurs the line between true media and maliciously fabricated generated media.

Aim to develop a robust classifier of news to eradicate instances of panic and confusion caused by fake news.

User friendly interactive web-application that links users to our classifier present in our web server.
